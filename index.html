Introduction介绍

This document serves as a reference for Custom Difficulty 2, a mod for Deep Rock Galactic which allows the creation of new hazards, or difficulties. Developed by @TheBrain as a successor to the Custom Difficulty mod, CD2 features many improvements over the flexibility and possibilities offered to difficulty designers:

本文档是深岩银河自定义难度2模组的参考文档,它允许你创建新的难度。CD2由@Brain开发，作为Cusotm Difficulty（CD1）的迭代版本，相比CD1拥有更高的灵活性和更多的可能

  • Allows players to a have a much finer control over many aspects of the gameplay by introducing mutators, control flow structures similar to those found in common programming languages.
  • 通过引入类似编程结构的 Mutators，让玩家可以更细致地控制游戏逻辑。
  • Exposes many enemy controls to change their behaviour, spawning mechanics and even aspect by featuring custom materials replication to clients.
  • 开放大量敌人控制项，可自由修改它们的行为、生成机制，甚至外观。
  • Allows manipulating the enemy pools live during a mission based on gameplay conditions.
  • 也允许在任务中即时调整敌人池。
  • Offers many features not available in CD1 such as controls for illumination, player scale, the amount of nitra in the map, etc.
  • 加入了很多CD1没有的功能，比如光照、玩家体型、地图硝石数量的控制功能等等
  • ... and many others.以及更多内容
